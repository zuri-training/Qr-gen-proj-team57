What is a QR Code?

A QRCode is a 2-D square-shaped black and white bar code that can be read by smartphones. A QRCode can store up to 4296 characters or 7089 digits in a single code making it viable to hold large amouts of content.


What features does GenCode API gives access to?

  • Colored QRCode Generation

    • Instead of the regular black and white, you can add some colors to your QRCode to make it more attractive. You just have to select your preferred colors. Beware that using lighter background-color for your QRCode disrupt some QRCode Scanner from scanning

  • More download formats

    • After generating your QRCode, your Qrcode is available for download in these formats:PDF,PNG and JPEG.

  • Share QRCode

    • You can also share your code across your preferred social media platform.
